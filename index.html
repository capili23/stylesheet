Side-Bar

Ah, Judge Holden! People often describe him in *Blood Meridian* as a terrifying and almost otherworldly figure. He’s the kind of character who’s both philosophically complex and completely chilling. Many editors and scholars focus on his almost godlike presence, using phrases like “the embodiment of violence” or “the representation of evil in its purest form.” Some say he's the perfect antihero, though he’s more of a chaotic force, leaving destruction in his wake.

So, in short, Judge Holden is typically explained as a kind of unstoppable, godlike force of violence and chaos, making him fascinating for people to explore in edits and memes. It's wild how people can romanticize a character who's basically an incarnation of destruction!
